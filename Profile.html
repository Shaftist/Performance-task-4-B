Profile


「Mission」

Madrasah is a Community-Based Institution that aims to give quality education in the Arabic language and Islam.
It also Serves as a place to foster peace and understanding between the Muslims and the Christians.


「History」

The madrasah or Islamic education in the Philippines is believed to coincide with the growth and coming of Islam,
which was brought by Arab missionaries and Malay adventurers who settled in Sulu and western Mindanao.
